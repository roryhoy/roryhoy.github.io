Biofeedback Crystallization

 · 2021

A satellite project part of Music Tech Fest Aveiro 2021 by Marcus Gordon, Rory Hoy, Kwame Kyei-Boateng, Grace Grothaus, Lucy Lacono, Filiz Eryilmaz, and Ilze Briede (Kavi).

Exploring biosignals, cymatics, live coding, dynamic visuals, and crystal formation, the week long event gave us space to jam to the festival's theme of "Just Ocean".
